# 💊 Automatic Pill Dispenser

## 🎯 Project Objective  
The **Automatic Pill Dispenser** was designed to help **elderly or chronically ill patients** take their medication on time without supervision.  
This **PIC18-based embedded system** automatically dispenses two types of medicines at preset times, ensuring accurate dosage and timely reminders.

---

## 💡 My Contributions   
- Developed the **firmware** in Embedded C for real-time clock scheduling, user alerts, and servo control.  
- Implemented **IR-based detection logic** to confirm successful pill dispensing and prevent duplication.  
- Created a **user-friendly interface** with an LCD display and keypad for easy configuration.  
- Integrated **buzzer and alert features** to assist elderly users with visual and audio notifications.  

---

## 🧰 Technologies & Tools Used  
- **Microcontroller:** PIC18 series  
- **Actuators:** Servo Motors (x2)  
- **Sensors:** IR Sensor for detection  
- **User Interface:** Keypad and LCD Display  
- **Development IDE:** MPLAB X  
- **Programming Language:** Embedded C
