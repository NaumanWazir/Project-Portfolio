## About Me  

I am an **Electronic Engineer** with over a decade of hands-on experience in **Embedded Hardware and Software Design**.  
I spent **13 years at the National Space Agency**, specializing in **Inertial and Integrated Navigation Systems**.  

Due to the sensitive and defense-related nature of these projects, I cannot share full source code, schematics, or detailed designs. Instead, I have prepared **high-level descriptions** to demonstrate:  
- The scope of my work  
- My role in each project  
- The technologies and skills I applied  

**Please note:**  
- The number of projects may appear limited, but each was a **long-term, high-complexity prototype** for space applications.  
- A single project often spanned **2–4 years**, requiring **rigorous design, testing, and iterative development** before reaching a flight-ready prototype.  
- The focus here is to give **recruiters and collaborators** insight into my experience and expertise.  

Since **2023**, I have also been working as a **Freelancer**, focusing on **commercial and consumer embedded projects**. These projects are included in this repository as well.  

Each project has its own folder with a dedicated **README.md** outlining:  
- Project objectives  
- My contributions  
- Technologies and tools used
