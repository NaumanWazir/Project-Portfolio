# Fault-Tolerant Integrated/Inertial Navigation System

This project aimed to develop a **fault-tolerant navigation system** for satellite launch vehicles, where **redundancy and reliability** are critical.  

## Background  

After returning from my MSc studies in 2018, I was appointed as the **Project Director** for this initiative. The design was inspired by the **RIFCA (Redundant Inertial Flight Control Assembly)** used in the **Delta Launch Vehicle**.  

The project required close collaboration across multiple specialized groups:  
- **Inertial System Design Lab** – Responsible for hardware design and testing.  
- **Navigation Scheme Design Lab** – Developed MATLAB-based fault detection and isolation (FDI) algorithms using a **6-accelerometer, 6-gyroscope skew configuration**.  
- **GPS Design Lab** – Worked on developing and integrating a **high-dynamic GPS receiver**.  
- **Mechanical Design Group** – Designed the system enclosure, mounting mechanisms, and provided environmental test support.  

## My Contribution  

As **Project Director**, I managed both the technical and organizational aspects of the project:  

- **Project Leadership**  
  - Coordinated efforts between diverse teams with differing priorities and technical backgrounds.  
  - Ensured smooth collaboration and timely integration of subsystems.  

- **Hardware Design**  
  - Directly contributed to **hardware schematics and PCB design** for the inertial subsystem.  

- **Embedded Implementation**  
  - Translated MATLAB-based **Fault Detection and Isolation (FDI) schemes** into **embedded C**.  
  - Implemented real-time redundancy management for multiple sensors (6 accelerometers and 6 gyros).  

## Outcome  

- Successfully designed and tested a **prototype fault-tolerant INS/Integrated Navigation System**.  
- Demonstrated robust operation under simulated fault conditions, validating redundancy and isolation schemes.  
- Provided a foundation for **reliable navigation systems in launch vehicle applications**, aligning with international best practices.  

## Technologies & Tools  

- **Embedded C (PIC32 microcontroller platform)**  
- **MATLAB-to-Embedded translation of FDI schemes**
